Kinokuniya

Are you a fan of anime or like reading manga? Come over to Kinokuniya here at Little Tokyo! They have such an abundance of manga and light novels, you'd be sure to fill up your collection fast! There are also other shops and stores that are sure to get you intrigued and excited!

Daikokuya

Hungry? Come to Daikokuya! Eat your fill of delicious meals and appetizers. Try their specialty dishes like their famous Daikoku Ramen, or try any of their big and fulfilling rice bowls! They also have many store specials to choose from ranging from tofu nuggets to thir spring veggie rolls! You will sure to be satisfied.

Nisei Week Festival

Come to the annual Nisei Week Festival and see an event you wouldn't be able to enjoy anywhere! Come see the parade with dancers performing historic choreographys, eye-widening parade floats. Enjoy delicious foods, drinks, and desserts! Click here more information of the festival. Trust me, this isn't an event you don't want to miss!

About
